About ARIAN

Rooted in the belief that every person is unique, we guide learners by aligning their interests, abilities, and aspirations. Through personalised career counselling, emotional counselling, scientific assessments, and NLP-based interventions, we bring clarity and transformation to decision-making. Our interactive workshops further enhance awareness, life skills, and personal growth. We work closely with students, parents, and educators to build a supportive ecosystem for holistic development. Our mission is to transform confusion into clarity and potential into purposeful achievement.

Services

1. Career Counselling

Personalised one-on-one sessions designed to help students understand their strengths, interests, and suitable career options. The focus is on clarity, confidence, and informed decision-making.

Who it is for: Students (Grade 8–12), College Graduates, Working Professionals

Mode: Online / Offline / Hybrid

2. Psychometric Assessment

A scientific evaluation of aptitude, personality, and interests to identify the most suitable career pathways. Includes a detailed report and expert interpretation.

Who it is for: Students (Grade 8–12), College Graduates, Working Professionals

Mode: Online / Hybrid

3. Stream Selection Guidance

Guidance for students at a crucial stage of choosing the right stream after Grade 10, based on their profile and future aspirations.

Who it is for: Students (Grade 9–10), College Graduates, Working Professionals

Mode: Online / Offline

4. Career Roadmap Planning

Step-by-step planning including courses, entrance exams, skill development, and long-term career pathways for selected fields.

Who it is for: Students (Grade 10–12), College Graduates, Working Professionals

Mode: Online / Hybrid

5. Workshops & Training Programs

Interactive and activity-based workshops designed to engage, inspire, and empower participants. These sessions focus on career awareness, life skills development, effective teaching methodologies, classroom management strategies, and emotional wellbeing.

Who it is for: Schools, Students, Parents, Teachers, Institutions

Mode: Online / Offline / Hybrid

6. Personal Counselling (Emotional & Academic Support)

Supportive counselling sessions addressing emotional wellbeing, stress, anxiety, motivation, academic challenges, and personal growth. Focused on building resilience and confidence.

Who it is for: Students, Parents, teachers, individuals

Mode: Online / Offline / Hybrid

7. NLP (Neuro-Linguistic Programming) Sessions

Transformational sessions using NLP techniques to help individuals overcome limiting beliefs, improve mindset, enhance communication, and achieve personal and professional goals.

Who it is for: Students, Parents, teachers, individuals

Mode: Online / Offline / Hybrid

About Founder

Akila Victoria Martin

Ms. Akila Victoria Martin is a distinguished educator, counsellor, career mentor, trainer, NLP practitioner, poet, and author with over two decades of experience in education, leadership, and emotional wellbeing. She has conducted more than 1,000 counselling sessions and facilitated over 200 workshops, impacting students, parents, and educators. She is the Founder of ARIAN (AK's Foundation), an initiative focused on nurturing academic excellence alongside emotional resilience. She currently serves as a Resource Person at Orient Blackswan, Career Counsellor at Mentoria, and Auditor & Trainer at Brilliant National School.
